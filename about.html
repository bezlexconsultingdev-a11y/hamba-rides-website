About Hamba Rides

Your Journey, Our Pride - Connecting South Africa, One Ride at a Time

Our Story

Hamba Rides was born from a simple vision: to revolutionize transportation in South Africa by making it safe, affordable, and accessible to everyone. Founded in 2026, we recognized the need for a locally-focused ride-hailing service that truly understands the unique needs of South African communities.

The name "Hamba" comes from the Zulu word meaning "to go" or "to travel" - perfectly capturing our mission to keep South Africa moving. We're not just another ride-hailing app; we're a proudly South African company committed to supporting local drivers, creating jobs, and building stronger communities.

🎯

Our Mission

To provide safe, reliable, and affordable transportation solutions that empower South African communities, create economic opportunities for drivers, and make every journey a positive experience.

🌟

Our Vision

To become South Africa's most trusted and preferred ride-hailing platform, setting the standard for safety, innovation, and community impact while supporting local economic growth.

💎

Our Values
