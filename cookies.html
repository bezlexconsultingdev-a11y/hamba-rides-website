Cookie Policy

Last Updated: March 25, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Hamba Rides (Pty) Ltd ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our website, use our mobile applications, or interact with our services.

This policy should be read together with our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website or use an application. Cookies help websites and apps remember information about your visit, such as your preferences, login status, and browsing history.

1.1 Types of Cookies

Session Cookies: These are temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser or app. They help us maintain your session as you navigate through our platform.

Persistent Cookies: These remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them. They help us remember your preferences and settings for future visits.

First-Party Cookies: These are set by Hamba Rides directly and can only be read by us.

Third-Party Cookies: These are set by our partners and service providers (e.g., analytics providers, advertising networks) and can be read by those third parties.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies for the following purposes:

2.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for our platform to function properly. They enable core functionality such as:

  • User authentication and account access
  • Security and fraud prevention
  • Load balancing and performance optimization
  • Remembering your preferences and settings
  • Maintaining your session as you navigate the platform

You cannot opt out of essential cookies as they are required for the platform to work. Disabling them will prevent you from using our services.

2.2 Performance and Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how users interact with our platform by collecting anonymous information about:

  • Pages visited and features used
  • Time spent on different pages
  • Navigation paths through the platform
  • Error messages and technical issues
  • Device and browser information
  • Geographic location (city/region level)

We use this information to improve our services, fix bugs, and enhance user experience.

Analytics Providers We Use:

  • Google Analytics
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Mixpanel

2.3 Functionality Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization, such as:

  • Remembering your language preference
  • Saving your favorite locations
  • Customizing the interface based on your preferences
  • Remembering your payment method selections
  • Providing personalized ride recommendations

If you disable these cookies, some features may not work as intended.

2.4 Advertising and Marketing Cookies

These cookies are used to deliver relevant advertisements and measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns. They help us:

  • Show you relevant ads on other websites and platforms
  • Limit the number of times you see an ad
  • Measure ad campaign performance
  • Understand which marketing channels are most effective
  • Provide personalized promotional offers

Advertising Partners We Work With:

  • Google Ads
  • Facebook Pixel
  • LinkedIn Ads
  • Twitter Ads

You can opt out of advertising cookies through your browser settings or the cookie consent banner.

2.5 Social Media Cookies

These cookies enable social media features and allow you to share content from our platform. They are set by social media platforms when you use their sharing features or interact with social media content on our site.

Social media platforms may use these cookies to track your browsing activity across different websites.

3. Other Tracking Technologies

In addition to cookies, we use other tracking technologies:

3.1 Web Beacons (Pixels)

Small transparent images embedded in web pages or emails that help us:

  • Track email open rates
  • Measure campaign effectiveness
  • Understand user behavior
  • Verify that content has been viewed

3.2 Local Storage

HTML5 local storage allows us to store data locally on your device, including:

  • User preferences and settings
  • Cached data for faster loading
  • Offline functionality

3.3 Mobile Device Identifiers

On mobile devices, we may collect:

  • Advertising ID (IDFA on iOS, AAID on Android)
  • Device ID
  • App instance ID

These identifiers help us provide personalized experiences and measure app performance.

3.4 Software Development Kits (SDKs)

Our mobile apps include third-party SDKs that may collect information about your device and app usage. These include:

  • Analytics SDKs (Google Analytics, Firebase)
  • Crash reporting SDKs (Crashlytics)
  • Advertising SDKs (Google AdMob, Facebook Audience Network)
  • Payment processing SDKs

5. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

5.1 Cookie Consent Banner

When you first visit our website, you'll see a cookie consent banner that allows you to:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Reject non-essential cookies
  • Customize your cookie preferences by category

You can change your preferences at any time by clicking the "Cookie Settings" link in the footer.

5.2 Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

  • Block all cookies
  • Block third-party cookies only
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Set cookies to be deleted when you close your browser
  • Receive notifications when cookies are set

How to manage cookies in popular browsers:

  • Google Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data

5.3 Mobile App Settings

On mobile devices, you can control tracking through:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking → Allow Apps to Request to Track
  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

You can also manage permissions within the Hamba Rides app settings.

5.4 Opt-Out Links

You can opt out of certain third-party cookies and tracking:

  • Google Analytics: Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • Google Ads: Google Ads Settings
  • Facebook: Facebook Ad Preferences
  • Network Advertising Initiative: NAI Opt-Out
  • Digital Advertising Alliance: DAA Opt-Out

6. Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you choose to disable or block cookies, please be aware that:

  • Some features of our platform may not work properly
  • You may need to re-enter your login credentials each time you visit
  • Your preferences and settings will not be saved
  • You may see less relevant advertisements
  • Some pages may load more slowly
  • Certain functionality may be unavailable

Note: Essential cookies cannot be disabled as they are required for the platform to function.

7. Do Not Track Signals

Some browsers have a "Do Not Track" (DNT) feature that signals to websites that you do not want your online activity tracked. Currently, there is no industry standard for how to respond to DNT signals.

At this time, our platform does not respond to DNT signals. However, you can control tracking through the cookie consent banner and browser settings as described above.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children through cookies or other tracking technologies.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with information, please contact us immediately at privacy@hambarides.co.za.

9. International Data Transfers

Some of our third-party service providers (such as Google Analytics and Facebook) may transfer and process cookie data outside of South Africa. These transfers are subject to appropriate safeguards to protect your information.

By using our platform, you consent to the transfer of cookie data to countries outside South Africa.

10. Updates to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, legal requirements, or our practices. We will notify you of material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on our website and in our app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Displaying a notification in the app or via email for significant changes

We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
