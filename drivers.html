Driver Requirements

Make sure you meet these requirements before applying

👤 Driver Requirements

  • At least 21 years old
  • Valid South African driver's license
  • Minimum 2 years driving experience
  • Clean criminal record
  • Professional Driving Permit (PrDP)
  • Good communication skills

🚗 Vehicle Requirements

  • 4-door sedan or SUV
  • Model year 2015 or newer
  • Good condition (no damage)
  • Valid vehicle license
  • Comprehensive insurance
  • Air conditioning

📄 Documents Needed

  • South African ID or passport
  • Driver's license (front & back)
  • Professional Driving Permit
  • Vehicle registration papers
  • Proof of insurance
  • Bank account details

✅ Background Checks

  • Criminal record check
  • Driving history verification
  • Reference checks
  • Vehicle inspection
  • Identity verification
  • Safety training completion

Driver FAQs

How much can I earn?

Earnings vary based on hours worked, location, and demand. On average, drivers earn R15,000-R25,000 per month working full-time. You keep 79% of all fares.

When do I get paid?

Payments are processed weekly, every Monday, directly to your bank account. You can track your earnings in real-time through the driver app.

Do I need my own car?

Yes, you need access to a qualifying vehicle (2015 or newer, 4-door sedan or SUV) with comprehensive insurance and valid registration.

What are the working hours?

There are no set hours! Drive whenever you want - mornings, evenings, weekends, or just a few hours a week. You're completely in control of your schedule.
