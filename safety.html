Our Safety Commitment

At Hamba Rides, safety isn't just a feature—it's the foundation of everything we do. We've built multiple layers of protection to ensure that every rider and driver feels secure, from the moment they open the app to when they reach their destination.

Our comprehensive safety approach includes rigorous driver screening, real-time monitoring, emergency response systems, and continuous safety education. We work closely with law enforcement and safety experts to stay ahead of potential risks.

🛡️ Safety Promise: Every ride is protected by our multi-layered safety system, 24/7 support team, and commitment to continuous improvement.

In-App Safety Tools

Powerful features at your fingertips

1

Share Trip

Share your real-time location and trip details with up to 5 trusted contacts. They can track your journey and receive automatic notifications when you arrive safely.

2

Trusted Contacts

Add family and friends as trusted contacts. They'll automatically receive trip notifications and can check on you anytime during your ride.

3

Safety Check-In

Our system monitors unusual route deviations or extended stops. If detected, we'll check in with you to ensure everything is okay.

4

Driver Details

See your driver's photo, name, vehicle details, license plate, and rating before they arrive. Verify everything matches before getting in.

5

PIN Verification

For added security, verify your driver with a unique 4-digit PIN before starting your ride. Never share this PIN with anyone.

6

Report Issues

Quickly report safety concerns, inappropriate behavior, or route issues directly in the app. Our team responds immediately to all reports.

Safety Tips

Best practices for a safe ride experience

Before Your Ride

  • ✓ Verify driver details match the app
  • ✓ Check the license plate number
  • ✓ Share your trip with contacts
  • ✓ Wait in a safe, well-lit area
  • ✓ Confirm the driver knows your name
  • ✓ Never share your PIN code

During Your Ride

  • ✓ Sit in the back seat
  • ✓ Wear your seatbelt
  • ✓ Follow the route on your app
  • ✓ Keep personal belongings secure
  • ✓ Trust your instincts
  • ✓ Use the SOS button if needed

After Your Ride

  • ✓ Check for belongings before exiting
  • ✓ Rate your driver honestly
  • ✓ Report any issues immediately
  • ✓ Save your receipt
  • ✓ Wait until safely inside before ending trip
  • ✓ Provide feedback to help improve

Community Guidelines

Respect and safety for everyone

🤝 Mutual Respect

Treat all riders and drivers with courtesy and respect. Discrimination, harassment, or abusive behavior is not tolerated.

🚭 No Smoking/Vaping

Smoking and vaping are prohibited in all vehicles to ensure a comfortable environment for everyone.

🍺 Zero Tolerance

Drivers must maintain zero blood alcohol level. Intoxicated or disruptive riders may be refused service.

👥 Passenger Limits

Do not exceed the vehicle's passenger capacity. All passengers must have seatbelts available.

🐕 Pet Policy

Service animals are always welcome. Other pets require driver approval and must be in carriers.

📱 Privacy Respect

Do not record drivers or riders without consent. Respect everyone's privacy and personal space.
