Safety Feature

Women-Only Rides

We're proud to offer South Africa's leading women-only ride service. Female riders can request female drivers for added comfort, safety, and peace of mind.

How It Works:

  1. Toggle the Option: Simply enable "Women-Only" when requesting your ride
  2. Get Matched: You'll be connected with a verified female driver
  3. Ride with Confidence: Enjoy your journey with added comfort and security
Available on both Economy and Standard rides at no extra cost

Why Choose Women-Only Rides:

  • Enhanced safety and comfort
  • Verified female drivers with background checks
  • Reduced harassment concerns
  • Cultural sensitivity and understanding
  • Perfect for solo female travelers
  • Late-night peace of mind
